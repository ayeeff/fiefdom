Strategic Assessment

This scenario demonstrates how geopolitical tensions can be weaponized to attack financial infrastructure. Sanae Takaichi's Taiwan statements—whether intentional or not—create diplomatic cover for actions that would otherwise constitute clear economic warfare. The yen carry trade's evolution from tactical arbitrage to systemic infrastructure creates both dependency and vulnerability that sits at the intersection of monetary policy, financial stability, and great power competition.

Key Takeaway: China gains the ability to impose broad costs on Western financial systems while maintaining plausible deniability—transforming what could be viewed as financial aggression into "measured response to Japanese provocations." The August 2024 event demonstrated the carry trade's systemic importance; a coordinated attack would be significantly more severe and prolonged.
