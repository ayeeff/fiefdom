## üéØ Strategic Rationale

### **Why This Makes Sense**

1. **MUSK's real power = executive branch, not market cap**
   - Adding defense primes (NOC, HII, TXT, Boeing) reflects his **budget authority** via DOGE
   - Overlapping tech stakes (Nvidia, Broadcom, Qualcomm, AMD, Intel) reflect xAI's compute needs
   - Total equity still smaller than others, but **policy leverage is asymmetric**

2. **STARGATE's moat = infrastructure, not ownership**
   - Reduced Nvidia/AWS stakes don't weaken the **Stargate project itself**
   - Oracle + SoftBank + Anthropic core remains 100% aligned
   - The $500B buildout is happening regardless of public market stakes

3. **META's dilemma = market power without political power**
   - Still largest equity base (~$11.4T) but **shut out of administration**
   - Walmart/Berkshire additions reflect "old economy" allies who aren't politically aligned with Trump
   - Long-term bet: EU norms + California regulations eventually become global standards
